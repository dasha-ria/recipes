# **Fried noodles**

## Ingredients

- 1 kg salmon / chicken
- ginger
- garlic
- spring onions
- 200 ml soy sauce
- 80 ml apple cider vinegar
- 35 g brown sugar

## Instructions

Prepare rice in the rice cooker.

Chop ginger, garlic, spring onions, and cut the salmon into vertical pieces, or chicken into cubes. Then prepare the teriyaki sauce (soy sauce, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar) and mix it with the ginger and garlic.

Fry the salmon / chicken until it turns golden brown and itâ€™s pretty cooked. Add the sauce, wait for the sauce to thicken and add the spring onions.
